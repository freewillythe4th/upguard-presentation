Current challenge

Moving from lifecycle and brand marketing to systematic product marketing with sustainable positioning

Three critical assumptions to test

Assumption 1: Branded cards increase sharing confidence measurably

Test method: Sharing frequency analysis—200 users, 30 days

Success criteria: 25% increase in sharing frequency

Pivot trigger: If <10% behavior change, optimize for more dramatic visual transformation

Assumption 2: End user viral adoption drives admin interest faster than top-down sales

Test method: Track user-initiated vs. admin-initiated team setups across 50 conversions

Success criteria: User-initiated shows 2x faster adoption completion rates

Pivot trigger: If admin-first shows higher lifetime value, adjust primary targeting

Assumption 3: Custom Fonts creates measurable viral conversation triggers

Test method: Baseline survey → Post-launch survey at 30 days comparing colleague inquiry rates

Success criteria: 40% increase in "how did you do this?" conversations vs. baseline

Pivot trigger: If <20% increase vs. baseline, increase visibility of branded elements on shared cards
